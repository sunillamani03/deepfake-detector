What is a Deepfake?

Deepfakes are synthetic media where a person's likeness is replaced with someone else's using artificial intelligence and deep learning techniques. They can be incredibly realistic and difficult to detect with the naked eye.

üö® Why Deepfakes are Dangerous

  • Misinformation: Spreading false information through fake videos
  • Identity Theft: Impersonating individuals for fraud
  • Political Manipulation: Creating fake political speeches
  • Financial Scams: Deceiving people for money
  • Reputation Damage: Harming someone's credibility

üõ° How to Spot Deepfakes

Visual Clues:

  • Unnatural eye movements or lack of blinking
  • Inconsistent lighting or shadows on the face
  • Blurry or distorted edges around the face
  • Mismatched skin tones
  • Unusual facial proportions

Audio Clues:

  • Voice doesn't match lip movements perfectly
  • Robotic or unnatural speech patterns
  • Background noise inconsistencies
